How to Set Up Okta

Follow these steps to set up your Okta account securely.

Step 1: Access Okta

Go to jumia.okta.com and click on "Sign In".

Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

Use your company email and password to log in.

Step 3: Set Up Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Follow the on-screen instructions to set up MFA using an authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator or Okta Verify).

Step 4: Confirm Your Setup

Check your registered email for confirmation and verify your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What if I forgot my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions.

❓ How do I change my MFA settings?

Go to your Okta account settings and update your MFA preferences.

❓ Who do I contact for support?

Need help? Contact IT Support at itsupport.ke@jumia.com
